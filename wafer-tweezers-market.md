<p><p>The wafer tweezers market consists of various types designed for handling delicate semiconductor wafers. PEEK tweezers, made from polyether ether ketone, offer excellent chemical resistance and durability, making them ideal for cleanroom environments. PPS tweezers, constructed from polyphenylene sulfide, provide high thermal stability and resilience, suitable for high-temperature applications. Metal tweezers, often made from stainless steel, are preferred for their strength and precision, though they require careful use to avoid contamination. Each type plays a vital role in semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
