<p><ul><li>Solid Masking Protective Film</li><li>Liquid Masking Protective Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Masking Protective Film market is categorized into two main types: solid masking protective film and liquid masking protective film. Solid masking films are pre-formed sheets that provide a durable barrier against dust, scratches, and contaminants during manufacturing and installation processes. Liquid masking films, on the other hand, are applied as coatings that dry to form a protective layer, offering flexibility and conformability over complex surfaces. Both types serve essential roles in safeguarding surfaces in various industries, including automotive, construction, and electronics.</p></p>
