<p><p>The racing bike helmet market is predominantly divided into two types: MTB helmets and road helmets. MTB helmets are designed for mountain biking, featuring enhanced protection and a more robust build to endure rugged terrains and impacts. They often include visors and increased ventilation for comfort. In contrast, road helmets prioritize aerodynamics and lightweight design for speed on paved surfaces. They typically have a sleek profile, minimal ventilation features, and are optimized for long-distance rides, focusing on performance and efficiency.</p></p>
