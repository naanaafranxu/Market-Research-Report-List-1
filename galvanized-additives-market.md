<p><p>The galvanized additives market is diverse, encompassing several product types vital for enhancing corrosion resistance in metal surfaces. Degreasing agents remove oils and contaminants, while cleaning additives ensure metals are free from dirt and residues. Inhibitors prevent corrosion during processing, and pickling additives prepare surfaces by removing oxide layers. Scaling powders manage mineral deposits, and passivators enhance the protective oxide layer on galvanized surfaces. Other additives further support various processes in the galvanization industry, ensuring optimal performance and longevity.</p></p>
