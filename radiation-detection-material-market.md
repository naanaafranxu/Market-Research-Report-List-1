<p><p>The radiation detection material market comprises three main types: semiconductors, scintillators, and others. </p><p>Semiconductors utilize materials like silicon and germanium to detect radiation through electron-hole pair generation, offering high resolution and compact designs. Scintillators emit light when exposed to radiation, enabling efficient detection and measurement, commonly used in medical imaging and safety applications. The "others" category includes various novel materials and technologies, such as gas detectors and optical detectors, catering to specific needs in industries like nuclear energy, environmental monitoring, and security.</p></p>
