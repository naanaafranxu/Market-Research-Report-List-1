<p><ul><li>Chronic Open Angle Glaucoma</li><li>Acute Closed Angle Glaucoma</li><li>Secondary Glaucoma</li><li>Normal-Tension Glaucoma</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glaucoma laser devices market encompasses various types of glaucoma, each requiring specific treatment approaches. Chronic open-angle glaucoma, the most common form, involves gradual vision loss, generally treated with laser therapies like Selective Laser Trabeculoplasty (SLT). Acute closed-angle glaucoma is a medical emergency, often addressed with laser peripheral iridotomy. Secondary glaucoma results from other eye conditions and may require targeted laser treatments. Normal-tension glaucoma, characterized by optic nerve damage despite normal pressure, can also benefit from laser interventions to protect vision.</p></p>
