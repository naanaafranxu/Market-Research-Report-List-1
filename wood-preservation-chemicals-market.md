<p><strong>The Wood Preservation Chemicals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-borne Preservatives</li><li>Oil-borne Preservatives</li><li>Light Organic Solvent Preservatives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wood preservation chemicals market is categorized into three main types: water-borne preservatives, oil-borne preservatives, and light organic solvent preservatives. Water-borne preservatives are eco-friendly, using water as a carrier for active ingredients, making them ideal for consumer use. Oil-borne preservatives contain oils that offer deep penetration and long-lasting protection against fungi and insects, typically used for outdoor applications. Light organic solvent preservatives utilize organic solvents to deliver wood protection, offering quick drying times and effective treatment, especially for delicate applications.</p></p>
