<p><p>The aluminium-extruded products market comprises three main types: mill-finished, anodized, and powder-coated. Mill-finished products have a natural, unprocessed appearance, showcasing the raw aluminium. Anodized products undergo an electrochemical process that enhances corrosion resistance and allows for a variety of colors and finishes, making them suitable for aesthetic applications. Powder-coated products are coated with a dry powder that, when heated, forms a durable and protective layer. Each type caters to distinct needs in construction, automotive, and consumer goods industries.</p></p>
