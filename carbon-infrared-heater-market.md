<p><p>The carbon infrared heater market consists of three types based on wavelength: short wave, medium wave, and long wave. Short wave heaters emit high-intensity infrared radiation, providing instant heat, ideal for large spaces. Medium wave heaters offer a balance between energy efficiency and warmth, suitable for diverse applications, including industrial and commercial use. Long wave heaters emit lower intensity infrared radiation, delivering gentle heat, primarily used in residential settings for comfort. Each type serves distinct needs across various sectors.</p></p>
