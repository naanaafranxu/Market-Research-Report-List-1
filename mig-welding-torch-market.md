<p><p>The MIG welding torch market is primarily classified into two types: water-cooled and air-cooled torches. Water-cooled torches use water circulation to dissipate heat, making them suitable for high-duty cycles and heavy welding applications, ensuring prolonged use without overheating. In contrast, air-cooled torches rely on ambient air for cooling, making them lighter and more portable, ideal for lighter tasks and occasional use. Both types serve distinct applications, balancing efficiency and convenience based on the welding demands.</p></p>
