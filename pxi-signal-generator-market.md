<p><p>The PXI signal generator market comprises three main types: Vector Signal Generators, RF Signal Sources, and Analog Signal Generators. Vector Signal Generators create complex modulated signals for testing communications systems, ensuring high fidelity in signal processing. RF Signal Sources focus on generating high-frequency signals used in wireless communications and radar systems, emphasizing stability and frequency range. Analog Signal Generators produce continuous waveforms for testing and simulating various electronic devices, highlighting versatility in signal types and applications in development and manufacturing.</p></p>
