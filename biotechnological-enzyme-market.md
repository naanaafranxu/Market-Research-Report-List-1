<p><p>The biotechnological enzyme market can be categorized into five main types: </p><p>1. **Hydrolases** break down compounds by adding water, playing pivotal roles in digestion and metabolism. </p><p>2. **Lyases** catalyze the addition or removal of groups to form double bonds, essential in various biosynthetic pathways. </p><p>3. **Isomerases** facilitate the rearrangement of molecular structures, crucial for metabolic processes. </p><p>4. **Ligases** join two molecules together, vital for DNA replication and repair. </p><p>5. **Others** encompass specialized enzymes with unique functions in diverse applications.</p></p>
