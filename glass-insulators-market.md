<p><strong>The Glass Insulators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Suspension Glass Insulators</li><li>Pin Glass Insulators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glass insulators market comprises various types, primarily suspension glass insulators and pin glass insulators. Suspension glass insulators are designed for overhead transmission lines, suspended from towers to maintain electrical isolation and support wires. They are ideal for high-voltage applications due to their durability and resistance to environmental factors. Pin glass insulators, on the other hand, are used on utility poles, where they are mounted directly to hold conductors in place. Both types ensure reliable electrical performance in power transmission systems.</p></p>
