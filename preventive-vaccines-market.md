<p><p>The preventive vaccines market comprises various administration methods, notably intramuscular injections, nasal sprays, and intradermal shots. Intramuscular injections deliver vaccines deep into muscle tissue, promoting robust immune responses. Nasal sprays provide a non-invasive option, targeting mucosal immunity through the respiratory tract. Intradermal shots involve administering vaccines into the skin's dermal layer, enhancing immune activation with smaller doses. Each method has distinct advantages, catering to different patient needs and preferences, ultimately contributing to broader vaccination coverage and disease prevention.</p></p>
