<p><p>The wooden biomass power generation market encompasses various technologies, primarily combustion, co-firing, and cogeneration. Combustion technology involves burning biomass to produce electricity, while co-firing combines biomass with fossil fuels in existing power plants to reduce carbon emissions. Cogeneration, or combined heat and power (CHP), generates both electricity and useful heat from biomass, enhancing energy efficiency. These technologies contribute to renewable energy goals, promote sustainable practices, and reduce reliance on fossil fuels while decreasing greenhouse gas emissions.</p></p>
