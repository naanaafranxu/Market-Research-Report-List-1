<p><p>The SSP (Single Super Phosphate) fertilizer market comprises two main types: SSP Powder and SSP Granulated. SSP Powder is a finely milled form, which allows for easy application and rapid nutrient availability to plants. It is typically used in smaller farms and gardens. In contrast, SSP Granulated is coarser, facilitating better handling and distribution with less dust. This form is preferred for larger agricultural operations, providing a controlled release of nutrients, enhancing soil fertility and crop yield efficiency.</p></p>
