<p><strong>The Oil Immersed Transformer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Paraffin based Transformer Oil</li><li>Naphtha based Transformer Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil-immersed transformer market features two primary types of transformer oils: paraffin-based and naphtha-based. Paraffin-based transformer oil, known for its high dielectric strength and thermal stability, offers superior insulation and cooling properties, making it widely preferred for high-voltage applications. In contrast, naphtha-based transformer oil is less commonly used but is valued for its cost-effectiveness and adequate performance in lower voltage scenarios. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring efficient operation and longevity of oil-immersed transformers in the energy sector.</p></p>
