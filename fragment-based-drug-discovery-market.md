<p><strong>The Fragment-based Drug Discovery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fragment Screening</li><li>Fragment Optimization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fragment-Based Drug Discovery (FBDD) encompasses two primary market types: Fragment Screening and Fragment Optimization. Fragment Screening involves identifying small chemical fragments that can bind to biological targets, utilizing techniques like X-ray crystallography and NMR spectroscopy. This initial stage helps in discovering potential leads. Fragment Optimization follows, where selected fragments are refined to enhance their binding affinity and pharmacological properties. This involves structure-based design and medicinal chemistry approaches to develop these fragments into viable drug candidates for clinical use.</p></p>
