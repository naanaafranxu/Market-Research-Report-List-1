<p><p>The plaque disclosing market includes three primary types: tablets, solutions, and swabs. Plaque disclosing tablets are chewed to reveal plaque on teeth through a temporary dye, making it easier for individuals to identify areas that require better brushing. Solutions are liquid applications swished in the mouth, similarly highlighting plaque. Swabs are disposable applicators soaked in disclosing agents, ideal for targeted application in dental settings. Each type serves as an effective tool in promoting oral hygiene by visually aiding plaque detection.</p></p>
