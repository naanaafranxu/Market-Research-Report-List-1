<p><p>The life support systems market encompasses various critical medical devices essential for patient care. Extracorporeal oxygenators facilitate oxygenation of blood outside the body, often used in surgical or critical care settings. Ventilators assist patients in breathing by providing mechanical support. Additionally, the "Others" category includes items like infusion pumps and dialysis machines, vital for comprehensive life support.</p></p>
