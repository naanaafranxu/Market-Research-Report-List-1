<p><p>SPECT systems can be categorized into hybrid SPECT and standalone SPECT markets. Hybrid SPECT combines single photon emission computed tomography with other imaging modalities, such as CT or MRI, providing enhanced diagnostic capabilities through more comprehensive anatomical and functional information. Both market types cater to different healthcare needs, with hybrid systems emphasizing advanced imaging and standalone systems prioritizing specialized nuclear diagnostics.</p></p>
