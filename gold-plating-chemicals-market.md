<p><p>The gold plating chemicals market is primarily classified into two types: cyanide gold plating chemicals and cyanide-free gold plating chemicals. Cyanide gold plating chemicals are traditional solutions that utilize cyanide compounds for gold deposition, offering superior quality and conductivity. However, they pose health and environmental risks. In contrast, cyanide-free gold plating chemicals provide a safer alternative, utilizing less toxic compounds while maintaining effective gold deposition. This shift towards cyanide-free options reflects growing regulatory and consumer demand for safer, environmentally friendly solutions in metal finishing.</p></p>
