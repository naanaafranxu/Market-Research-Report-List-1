<p><p>The metal zinc market primarily encompasses two segments: pure zinc and zinc alloys. Pure zinc is utilized in galvanization, providing corrosion resistance to steel and iron. It is integral in manufacturing die-cast parts for various industries. Zinc alloys, on the other hand, combine zinc with other metals like aluminum and copper, enhancing properties such as strength and casting fluidity. These alloys are commonly used in automotive, aerospace, and electronics applications, catering to diverse industrial needs and improving product durability.</p></p>
