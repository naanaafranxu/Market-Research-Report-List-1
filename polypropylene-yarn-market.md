<p><strong>The Polypropylene Yarn Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PP Multifilament</li><li>PP Monofilament</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polypropylene yarn market encompasses two main types: PP multifilament and PP monofilament. PP multifilament yarn consists of multiple filaments twisted together, offering flexibility and strength, making it suitable for textiles, industrial applications, and bags. In contrast, PP monofilament yarn is a single, sturdy filament, ideal for applications requiring durability, such as fishing lines and nets. Both types leverage polypropylene's lightweight properties and resistance to moisture, contributing to their demand across various industries.</p></p>
