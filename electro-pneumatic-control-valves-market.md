<p><strong>The Electro-Pneumatic Control Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ball Valve</li><li>Butterfly Valve</li><li>Gate Valve</li><li>Plug Valve</li><li>Piston Valve</li><li>Diaphragm Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electro-Pneumatic Control Valves market includes various valve types, each serving specific applications. Ball valves offer quick on-off control with minimal pressure drop. Butterfly valves are compact and efficient for regulating flow. Gate valves provide full flow by completely opening or closing the passage. Plug valves allow for smooth flow control and easy maintenance. Piston valves are used for high-pressure applications, providing precise flow regulation. Diaphragm valves are ideal for handling corrosive fluids and offer tight sealing capabilities. Each type fulfills distinct operational needs in industries.</p></p>
