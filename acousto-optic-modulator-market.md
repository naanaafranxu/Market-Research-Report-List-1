<p><ul><li>Fiber-Coupled Acousto-Optic Modulators</li><li>Free-Space Acousto-Optic Modulators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Acousto-Optic Modulator (AOM) market consists of two primary types: fiber-coupled AOMs and free-space AOMs. Fiber-coupled AOMs are designed to integrate seamlessly with optical fibers, enhancing performance in fiber optic communication systems. They offer high efficiency and are ideal for applications in telecommunications and laser systems. In contrast, free-space AOMs are utilized in setups where light travels through open air, often used in scientific research and industrial applications due to their versatility in handling varying beam paths.</p></p>
