<p><p>The fertilizer applicator market comprises three primary types: mounted, trailed, and self-propelled applicators. Mounted fertilizer applicators are attached directly to tractors, offering enhanced maneuverability for smaller fields. Trailed fertilizer applicators are towed behind tractors and are ideal for larger areas, providing flexibility and increased capacity. Self-propelled fertilizer applicators operate independently, equipped with their own engines, allowing for greater speed and efficiency in large farming operations. Each type caters to specific agricultural needs, enhancing productivity in fertilizer application.</p></p>
